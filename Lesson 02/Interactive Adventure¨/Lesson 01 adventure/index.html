You are a little kid who is terrified of water and proudly introduce yourself as a kid who never took a bath.

As you walk down the street, you suddenly hear a tiny voice screaming for help.

It seems to be coming from… a purple bar of soap on the ground!

What do you do?

Option 1: Ignore and walk away. Soap isn't supposed to talk.

Option 2: You look around, just in case the voice is coming from something else.
