You ask the soap: "Where do you want me to put you? I don't like soap... not even the kind that can talk!"

The voice responds: "I'm not a talking soap! I'm a genie who is trapped inside this soap! If you release me, I can grant you all your wishes! I can make you rich! Famous! You just need to use the soap until I'm free!"

To use the soap... that means taking a shower. Or worse. Multiple showers.

Your soul leaves your body for a second.

What do you do?

Option A: Not amount of money or fame is worth enduring a shower. You drop the soap on the ground and run away.
Option B: You start thinking about all the things you could wish for: toys, candy, video games... You decide to take the soap with you.
